# Idle RPG Gameplay

## **Core Mechanics**

### **Idle Rewards**:

* Players earn rewards over time without needing constant interaction.
* Use `/claim` to collect accumulated rewards like Gold, Miru Points, or items.

### **Simplified Commands**

The system relies on simple commands, such as:

* `/start`: Begin your adventure.
* `/battle`: Engage in combat.
* `/mine`, `/fish`, etc.: Perform skill-based activities.

### **Rebirth System**

After reaching Level 100, players can rebirth:

* Reduce XP required per level by 5%.
* Increase the max level cap by 5.
