# Game Mechanics

Miruka is an RPG-focused Discord bot where your character's power is determined by a combination of stats, equipment, and level. Understanding these mechanics is key to progressing in the world.

## 📊 Character Stats

Each adventurer has four core attributes that affect combat and gathering performance:

| Stat | Description | Primary Use |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **HP (Health Points)** | Your total vitality. Reaching 0 means defeat. | Combat Survival |
| **ATK (Attack)** | Determines the damage you deal to monsters. | Combat Damage |
| **DEF (Defense)** | Reduces incoming damage from enemies. | Damage Reduction |
| **LUK (Luck)** | Affects critical hit chance and rare item drop rates. | Loot & Crits |

---

## 📈 Leveling System

Experience (XP) is gained through almost every interaction in the game:
*   **Gathering**: `/chop`, `/fish`, `/mine`, `/hunt` provide small amounts of XP.
*   **Quests**: Completing missions via `/quest` grants significant XP bonuses.
*   **Combat**: Defeating enemies in various areas.

As you level up, your base stats increase automatically, allowing you to access more dangerous areas (`/area`).

---

## 💎 Item Rarity

Items in Miruka follow a strict rarity hierarchy. Higher rarity items provide better stat bonuses and are more valuable in the `/shop`.

| Rarity | Color Code | Drop Rate |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Common** | White | High |
| **Rare** | Blue | Medium |
| **Epic** | Purple | Low |
| **Legendary** | Gold | Very Low |
| **Mythical** | Crimson | Extremely Rare |

---

## 🛠️ Equipment

You can manage your gear using the `/equipment` command. 
*   **Slots**: Weapon, Armor, Accessory.
*   **Upgrades**: Most equipment can be upgraded to enhance its base stats.

_More mechanics like Elemental Damage and Guild Buffs are coming in future updates._
